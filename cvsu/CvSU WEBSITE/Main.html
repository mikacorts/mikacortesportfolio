ABOUT US

MANDATE:

Section 2 of Republic Act No. 8468 “An Act Converting the Don Severino Agricultural College in the Municipality of Indang, Province of Cavite into a State University, to be Known as the Cavite State University” states that, “The University shall primarily provide advance instruction and professional training in agriculture, science and technology, education and other related fields, undertake research and extension services, and provide progressive leadership in these areas.”

VISION:

The premier university in historic Cavite globally recognized for excellence in character development, academics, research, innovation and sustainable community engagement.

MISSION:

Cavite State University shall provide excellent, equitable and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences and technology through quality instruction and responsive research and development activities. It shall produce professional, skilled and morally upright individuals for global competitiveness.

CvSU - Bacoor City Campus

Cavite State University Bacoor Campus is a public university located in the city of Bacoor in the province of Cavite, Philippines. It is one of the seven campuses of the Cavite State University system.

In addition to its academic programs, Cavite State University Bacoor Campus also offers a vibrant student life with a variety of extracurricular activities. The campus is home to a number of student organizations and clubs. CvSu Bacoor Campus bears a strong commitment to research and innovation, its graduates highly sought-after by employers both in the Philippines and abroad.
